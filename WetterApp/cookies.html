What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work or function more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site.

How do we use cookies?

We use cookies for several purposes:

  1. Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable you to navigate the website and use its features.
  2. Analytics Cookies: We use these cookies to collect information about how visitors use our website. This information is used to analyze site usage and improve our website's content and user experience.
  3. Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements and measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns.

Third-party cookies

We may also use third-party cookies to analyze the usage of our website and for advertising purposes. These third-party cookies are governed by the respective privacy policies of the companies providing them.

Managing cookies

You can control and manage cookies in your browser settings. Most web browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies, or to receive a notification before a cookie is placed. Please refer to your browser's help documentation to learn more about how to manage cookies.
